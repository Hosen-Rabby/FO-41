Twelve Pillars

"Twelve Pillars" is a motivational and self-help book that offers twelve core principles for achieving success and personal development. It tells the story of a young man, Michael, who meets a successful entrepreneur named Charlie and learns valuable life lessons through their conversations. The book emphasizes various principles, including taking responsibility for one's life, setting goals, taking initiative, and building strong relationships.

Incorporating the principles from "Twelve Pillars" into my life as I pursue my Computer Science degree can help me develop a proactive and goal-oriented mindset. It encourages me to take ownership of my education and build meaningful connections with others in the field. These qualities can lead to personal growth, academic success, and a strong foundation for my future career.
